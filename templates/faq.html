How do I apply for lectures, as 신입생 or 재학생?

First year students apply as 신입생, others – 재학생.

How do I apply for lectures, as 신입생 or 재학생?

First year students apply as 신입생, others – 재학생.

Can you apply for part payment, what are the conditions?

Part payment is available only when you have to pay more than 1 mil won, it’s divided by 1 mil; e.g., Gotta pay 1.5 mil -> 1 part: 1 mil, 2 part: 500k.

How to pay 졸업준비금?

Payment is made via online bank transfer.

When you apply for lectures, why are there already subjects saying 만석?

Some subjects are meant for first-year students only, so you can apply for them only during 변경기간.

What papers do one require to get part-work permit?

Check the immigration office website or Kwangwoon news feed for the required documents.
